Fruit Smoothie

Smoothies are a great meal replacement when you are on the go and don't have time to make a proper breakfast, lunch, or dinner, fruit smoothies are a delicious way to get your daily fruits and veggies as well as protein and energy boost.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bag frozen mixed fruit
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup spinach

Steps

  1. Add everything to a blender and blend until smooth, enjoy.

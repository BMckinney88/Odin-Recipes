Whole Wheat Pancakes

Pancakes are an easy breakfast to make when you don't feel like really cooking anything, using whole wheat flour instead of all purpose can be a bit more healthy for the person making them, these can also be frozen and reheated in the oven for quick meals in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Add flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a medium bowl, mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl add milk, vanilla, and egg mix together, then add to flour mixture, if batter is too thick add more milk, if too runny add more flour.

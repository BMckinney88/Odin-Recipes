Brown Rice Stir Fry

Stir fry made with brown rice can be delicious healthy meal for those trying to eat clean, this can be made with or without meat depending on your preference, also a great meal prep meal that can be made at the beginning of the week and eating all week long with slight variations.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 bag stir fry mix
  • 1/4 cup stir fry sauce
  • salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. In a medium pot add 1 tbsp of butter, once melted add rice and stir for 2 minutes until rice is slightly toasted (keep a close eye on the rice as it can burn fast.) then add chicken broth and cook on low for 30 minutes with lid on completely.
  2. While rice is cooking add butter and oil to medium skillet, then add stir fry mix and cook for 10 minutes, adding more oil or butter as needed to prevent sticking.
  3. Once stir fry mix is almost done cooking add sauce and mix well making sue everything is coated with the sauce, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add cooked rice to a plate and cover with stir fry mix and enjoy hot; if meal prepping add rice and stir fry mix to a freezer safe container and freeze up to a month or place in refrigerator to heat and eat later in the week.
